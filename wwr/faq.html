Frequently Asked Questions:

Is white water rafting safe?
Yes, so long as you follow the safty precautions. Our team will instruct you on safe practices with White water rafting that will keep you, your friends, and your family safe while having fun on the water!
I have young children in my family, can they go rafting?
Yes, however, there are age (must be 10 years or older) and weight restrictions (dependant on raft), so please check into the details for the trip you are interested in, and contact us with any questions or concerns.
Can I bring alcohol?
For safety reasons, alcohol is not allowed before or during a river trip. For our clients safety, we may refuse service to anyone we believe is under the influence of drugs and/or alcohol.
Do I have to sign a waiver?
Yes, we require each participant joining us on the river to sign a waiver. Participants under the age of 18, must have their waiver signed by a parent or guardian.
What is your cancellation policy?
We do not provide cash refunds for cancellations under 14 days prior to your trip. Due to state regulations and tight permit allotments, once your space is confirmed, others are turned away.
Can I bring my own gear?
Yes, you are allowed to bring your own gear. Anything you may be missing can be rented from us as well.
